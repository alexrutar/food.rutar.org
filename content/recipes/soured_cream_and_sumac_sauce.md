100g soured cream

150g Greek yoghurt

juice and zest of ½ lemon

1 small garlic clove, crushed

1½ tbsp olive oil

1 tbsp sumac

½ tsp salt

¼ tsp black pepper
<!-- sep -->
Combine all the ingredients in a small bowl, mix well, and let sit for 30m.
